# Experience

## [KarmaPulse](https://karmapulse.com/) Startup — Nov 2017 - present; remote

Lastests products I worked on:

[SIUCAM](https://agua.conahcyt.mx/cuencas/) (2020 - present)
:    A national wide public platform to make information about water in Mexico accesible and colaborative.
As a Product Manager I had to:

- Create a consistent data storytelling with **40+ datasets** about quality, quantity, locations, uses and sociocultural information about water in Mexico,
from local monitoring to big government data
- Manage expectations and competing priorities between **50+ stakeholders**, from all levels of government to local activists and academics
- Face accesibility challenges: from infrastructure to a wide range of data literacy contexts, because in data apps
**"If people can't understand data, then it's not accesible or usable"**. I facilitated multiple techniques of user validation an testing.

*AI Comment Classification Dashboard* (2024 - present)
:    Process of RAG + Prompt Engineering to infer predefined custom categories from social media comments and visualize results in a Data App.

- We deployed a **fully functional hi-fi prototype in 5 days**
- We tested it with paying customers in order to implement a co-creation program to get continuous improvement of marketing reporting results.

*Web Reports* (2023 - present)
:    A set of interoperable products made to facilitate human comprehension of social data and trigger data sensemaking
. As a PM, I had to:

- Set the vision aligned with business strategy, usability testing and technical viability.
- This is currently KarmaPulse's main product, **used by 50% of our B2B customers**, it has helped to extend average **Life Time Value** (from 12 to 16 months) and decrease **Customer Acquisition Cost** for clients that looked for social data analysis.
